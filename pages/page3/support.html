Support Us

TutoArt is built by students, for students. Some of our tutorials are handpicked from YouTube (the ones we found super helpful!), and some are made by us to guide fellow beginners. We try our best to make everything simple, creative, and beginner-friendly.

If you find our content useful and want to help us grow, here's how you can support us:

  • Share TutoArt - Tell your friends, classmates, or post about us online! It helps more people discover what we offer.
  • Send Feedback - We'd love to hear what you think. Your suggestions help us improve and add better content. Email us at tutoart@gmail.com.
  • Unlock More Content - Sign up to access intermediate and advanced tutorials. It helps us maintain the site and continue creating.
  • Become a Member - Support us further and get exclusive content, features, or early access to new lessons.
