About us

TutoArt is an online learning space made by student artists who know how it feels to start from scratch. We created this website to help beginners learn the basics of art through simple, step-by-step tutorials. Whether you're just discovering your passion or want to improve your skills, we're here to guide you.

Our beginner lessons are free and easy to access, while intermediate and advanced content is available for those who want to explore more by signing up. We aim to make art learning fun, approachable, and something you can enjoy at your own pace.

At TutoArt, we believe that anyone can learn and grow as an artist — all you need is the right guidance and a space that understands your journey.
